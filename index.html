    MIT's Autonomous Robotic Competition Team

    We are MIT's autonomous robotics competition team

    Arcturus is building an autonomous boat for the 2022 RoboBoat competition taking place this June

    Arcturus is the team representing the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in the RoboBoat competition.

    • What: RoboBoat is an international competition where students build and program an autonomous boat to navigate through a challenge course.
    • Where: Nathan Benderson Park, Sarasota, Florida
    • When: June 20-25, 2022

    This is our first year competing, and we are very excited to get off to a strong start. Want to learn more about what we're up to? Check out our blogs!
